Peaceful Nature in my Garden

My garden is a peaceful place where I feel calm and relaxed. The gentle breeze and warm sunlight make it a perfect place to spend my time.

In the early morning, birds sing sweet songs as the sun slowly rises. Their melodies fill the air with joy and beauty.

Garden Rules

Our garden is a peaceful community space. To keep it clean and beautiful, please follow the rules below:

  • Do not pick flowers or damage any plants.
  • Throw garbage only in the trash bins.
  • Be respectful and kind to others.
  • No loud music or noise in the garden.
  • Children must be guided by adults.
